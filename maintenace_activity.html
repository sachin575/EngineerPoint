Maintenace

       The dictionary meaning of maintenance is to keep something in working condition. Maintenance must be done to maintain machine in working condition. To make machine investment profitable, increase productivity, reduce breakdowns, maintenance activity is carried out. Although, breakdown cannot be avoided but their frequency can be reduced by planned maintenance.

Maintenace definition as per DIN 31051

        Measure for preserving and restoring the desired condition and for determining and assessing the actual condition of technical system.

Maintenance Types

Inspection

    Activities and process to determine the actual status and to derive conclusion for future use. Its main objective is to detect occurance of damage and its solutions in advance.

      Inspection activities involve determining leakage, cracks, vibrations, temperature, humidity, resistance, voltage etc.

Servicing

    Activities that delay the material wearing process and increase its life is called servicing. it primarily focus on increasing material life by adding auxilary material if needed, replacement etc.

       Servicing activities involves lubricating, refilling of auxilary material, replacing small parts, readjusting position, cleaning etc.

Repair

On the basis of inspection repair activities are planned. It mainly focuses on removing faulty eqiupment or removing fault in the equipment. Repair restore the wear margin without and technical improvement.

Improvement

After inspection it came to be known that repair is needed, repairing is done still there are frequent breakdown, faults or error. At this stage improvement comes into the picture

Improvement increases the wear margin as compared to initial wear margin. Improvement and repair are most cost intensive part of the maintenance
